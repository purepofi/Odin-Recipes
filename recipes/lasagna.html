Lasagna

The lasagna plate is a delicious creation of the Italians which layers pasta, tomatoe sauce, cheese, and meat.

There are many versions of lasagna, for example, the image above depicts a beautiful merge between Italian and Mexican cuisine, combining the famous mexican dish "Enchiladas" with the Italian Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta in boiling water
  2. Warm up tomatoe sauce
  3. Add desired spices to tomatoe sauce
  4. Place a layer of tomatoe sauce at the bottom of tray
  5. Layer cooked pasta and cheese, adding a layer of tomatoe sauce between each one
