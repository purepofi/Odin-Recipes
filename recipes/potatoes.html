Air Fryer Potatoe Wedges

Potatoe Wedges are an easy, quick, and delicious

This is great recipe for satiating the tummy on a busy evening

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
